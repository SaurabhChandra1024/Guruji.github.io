ISKCON Lucknow Temple

Construction began in 2015 and continues under the guidance of H.G Aparimay Shyam Das Ji. The temple's framework is a testament to the dedication of devotees and the tireless supervision of Guruji. As completion nears, anticipation grows for this grand spiritual hub in Lucknow.

"Bhakti Sanatan Dharm Ka Aadhaar"

This seminal work by HG Aparimaya Shyam Das explores devotion as the foundation of all spiritual traditions. Through scriptural analysis and philosophical insight, the book highlights the universal principles of bhakti and its role in spiritual growth and self-realization.

Meeting with Government Officials

HG Aparimaya Shyam Das Ji met with senior Uttar Pradesh officials to discuss integrating spiritual and cultural values into governance and education, aiming to foster a harmonious, values-based society.

Prabhupad Youth Army

Established on August 15, 2023, the Prabhupad Youth Army (PYA) nurtures spiritual growth among youth, guided by H.G. Aparimay Shyam Das Ji. The program empowers young minds with values, compassion, and responsibility rooted in Srila Prabhupada's teachings.
