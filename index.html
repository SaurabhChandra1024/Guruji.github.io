ISKCON Lucknow Temple

The construction, initiated in 2015, remains an ongoing endeavor under the vigilant supervision of H.G Aparimay Shyam Das Ji. Despite the passage of time, the project continues to progress, with the temple's framework gradually taking shape. The construction site is a testament to the unwavering dedication of the devotees and the unrelenting efforts of H.G Aparimay Shyam Das Ji, who oversees every aspect of the project. As the temple's completion draws near, the excitement among devotees grows, and the anticipation of a grand spiritual hub in Lucknow becomes increasingly palpable, awaiting the day of its grand inauguration.

"Bhakti Sanatan Dharm Ka Aadhaar"

"Bhakti Sanatan Dharm Ka Aadhaar" is a seminal work penned by HG Aparimaya Shyam Das, a distinguished scholar and devotee of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON). This treatise provides an in-depth examination of the role of devotion as the fundamental basis of all spiritual traditions. Through a meticulous analysis of scriptural references and philosophical frameworks, the author elucidates the universal principles of bhakti, or loving devotion, and its significance in achieving spiritual growth and self-realization. This book offers a profound and insightful exploration of the human condition, making it a valuable resource for scholars, spiritual seekers, and devotees.

Meeting with Government Officials

HG Aparimaya Shyam Das ji, a renowned ISKCON devotee, met with High-ranking government officials of the Uttar Pradesh government to discuss the promotion of spiritual and cultural values. The meeting aimed to explore ways to integrate spirituality into governance and education, fostering a more harmonious and values-based society.

Prabhupad Youth Army

The Prabhupad Youth Army (PYA) was established on August 15, 2023, to promote spiritual development among young minds. Guided by H.G. Aparimay Shyamdas Ji, the PYA offers a comprehensive curriculum rooted in Srila Prabhupada's teachings. The program aims to empower youth with spiritual values, compassion, and responsibility.
